Privacy Policy for Coda Barcode Scanner

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Coda ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our barcode scanning application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Barcode Data: The content of barcodes you scan or manually create
  • Titles, Tags, and Notes: Optional metadata you add to your saved barcodes
  • Timestamps: When barcodes are scanned or created

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT store camera images or photos
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Barcode scans are saved to help you organize and access them later
  • Camera access is used solely for real-time barcode scanning
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All barcode data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored in Android Keystore (hardware-backed when available)
  • Data is protected even if your device is compromised
  • Root detection warns users of potential security risks

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your saved barcodes anytime
  • Delete: Remove individual barcodes or all data
  • Export: Your data never leaves your device
  • No Account Required: No registration or login needed

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

Camera Permission (Required for Scanning)

  • Used only for real-time barcode scanning
  • No photos or videos are captured or stored
  • You can deny this permission and still use manual barcode creation

Vibrate Permission (Optional)

  • Provides haptic feedback when barcodes are detected
  • Purely for user experience enhancement

Data Retention

  • Data is stored indefinitely on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently
  • No backups are created outside your device

Children's Privacy

Coda does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Open the Codes page
  2. Swipe left on any barcode to delete it
  3. To delete all data, uninstall the app

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data practices:

  • Email: privacy@codaapp.com
  • This is a local-only app with no data collection, but we're happy to address any concerns

Third-Party Services

Coda uses the following third-party libraries:

  • Google ML Kit: For barcode detection (on-device processing only)
  • ZXing: For barcode generation (local processing only)

Neither service transmits your data to external servers.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Android privacy best practices
  • GDPR principles (though no personal data is collected)
  • CCPA requirements (no data sale or sharing)

Summary

Coda is a privacy-first barcode scanner. All your data stays on your device, encrypted and secure. We don't collect, transmit, or share any information.
